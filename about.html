About Treats by Bee

Treats by Bee is a homemade treats business created with love and passion. We offer a variety of delicious goodies made to brighten up your day! We specialize in custom-made treats for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, and every special occasion.

Our Mission

To make your events sweeter with handmade, beautifully presented treats that taste just as good as they look.

Meet Bee

Hi! I’m Bee, a self-taught baker from Benoni, South Africa. What started as a hobby turned into a full-blown business, and I’m so excited to share it with you!
